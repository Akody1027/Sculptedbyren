Spray tans at SBR are formulated to counter orange undertones. They're customized to match your skin and meet your tanning goals. You can breathe easy with our premier collection of sunless products, you'll have complete control of your look.

Some self-tanners (not ours!) can leave unsightly streaks, but when you spray at SBR you'll receive a flawless even tan. You'll have a little prep before: exfoliate, shave and make sure you're moisturizer is designed for sunless tanning. If you're looking for a moisturizer, our products have you covered!

Add a fragrance to your spray tan session and say goodbye to the post spray smell you'll step out of the booth feeling great and smelling like.. Well, that depends on what fragrance you choose!

Dihydroxyacetone (DHA for short) is the active ingredient in all sunless tanners. It’s a colorless, natural, plant-derived compound that has been approved by the FDA for external application for over 50 years. Sculpted By Ren uses only eco-certified DHA solutions, and we provide protective eyewear, nose filters and lip balm. Additionally, all of our spray tan systems are cleaned and sanitized immediately after each session.

Wearing dark, loose-fitting clothes to your spray tan is best. If possible, wear nail polish—even if your nail polish is a clear coat, it’ll help protect your nails from discoloration. Avoid wearing perfumes, deodorant or makeup that can create a barrier for absorption. Tight-fitting clothing and shoes such as boots, socks or tights might rub off some of the spray tan and cause uneven results—it’s best to be safe and avoid these!
Avoid bathing, swimming or engaging in activities that work up a sweat for at least 4 hours. Because chlorine can quickly break down a spray tan, we recommend avoiding it entirely if possible.
